Game Design Part 9

今天开始第三阶段的课程：World Design for Video Games，第一节课感觉一大波问题来袭、、在我们自己玩游戏的时候，除了身为玩家去享受那个游戏之外，还要作为一个游戏设计者，这个游戏设计背后的原因。下面是一下可以思考的方向~

第九周课程：What is a Game World

Properties and Rules

  • Under what properties and rules are these video game structured? What defines a game world?
  • Think about gameplay mechanics.
  • In the game world, player is playing as a stranger in a strange world, or a person who has lived his whole life?
  • How are the crews in the world ganna interact with that world?
  • Why is the world appealing to you?

Story, the Vertical Spine

The story telling is truly the engine of the game.

  • Why this world? Why start from this place?
  • Is the entrie world going to be the playground?
  • Is there a bigger outer world? Is that playable area? Why? How this world and outer world will affact to each other?
  • Will your actions have an impact to the world? How?
  • How are you going to leave a mark in that world?

Making a Game World

  • Look at well designed games, fantasy novels, TV shows and movies, will you want to maybe have an impact on them? Play with them? Play in them?
  • Natural landscape, monument, why is it so attractive? How can you translate that in video game worlds? What feeling can you get from this?
  • Follow your instinct when you play video game, and try to define why you like being in this world.

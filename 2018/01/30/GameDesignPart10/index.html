Game Design Part 10

第十周课程：Do Your Research

Don’t Create from Void

不要凭空创造，不仅仅是游戏，其实任何设计，创意，都不应该是凭空出现的。搜寻电影，书，画，行为艺术，也可以只是你家附近，发现其中能让你感兴趣的部分，分析让你感兴趣的点在哪里。
观察周围的各种活动，运动，音乐，声音，材料，氛围，去辨别他们是什么样子的，是如何进行的，是怎么变化的。足够了解你现在的世界，才能更好的创造另一个世界。
Play with everything.

Aesthetics & Gameplay

  • Make your gameplay and aesthetic complimentary
  • Video games are tactile.
    the game world will give you feedback. Think about how will you interact with the world.
    e.g. If the world is in clay, will you be able to remodle, to change, to use this clay property?

Clockworks

Think about the inner mechanics of the world.

在没有玩家的世界里，它本身是怎么运作的？比如这是一个有着悠久历史的世界，它有不同的种族，各种各样的事件，冲突，战争；或者它是一个原始世界，甚至没有人类的足迹，一切都只是遵循自然的规律。

  • Where is your world located?
  • What composes it? (water? space? desert?)
  • Why does it look in this way?
  • What about its inhabitants?
  • What lies at the core of your world’s existence?
  • Is your world really what is seems?

Rulebook

  • Think about what drives the player in the game.
  • What can a player do here?

Make it yours

  • Make the world yours, even if it is adapted from existing one.
  • Bring your own artistry to it.
  • Keep what matters the most, anything that still works even without any style, design, imagery, then make the design.

Shuffle your ideas

Get everything you have, break them into chunks, break chunks into bits, then gather them all around, shuffle them, bring new things, figure out what connects what, what triggers what.

When everything is done, do it again.

Strategies for shuffling your ideas

Cut-outs:

  • Write your ideas out on small pieces of paper, like a Post-It. Keep every idea very succinct and textual.
  • Organize your ideas thematically, then use a board to pin them up or sort them into boxes.

Create a mood board:

  • Organize and display visual inspirations, like a physical Pinterest board or Tumblr.
  • Cut out images and clippings from various sources, like old magazines.

Sketch in a notebook, sketchbook, or digitally:

  • Sketches can then be added to your mood board.

If most of your research is digital:

  • Use Pinterest to organize visuals.
  • Use Evernote for both text and visual.
  • Or, software used to generate business processes or site maps can be used with some success for your own game world maps. Try free, cloud-based options like Coggle or Gliffy.
